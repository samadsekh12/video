A. General Awareness – 20 Marks

Questions will be designed to test the candidate’s General Awareness on the following topics:

1. Indian History and Geography


2. Knowledge on Indian Constitution


3. NEP at school level and school level education in West Bengal


4. Gender awareness – Rules and Regulations


5. Elementary knowledge about the traditional and contemporary popular culture of India


6. Basic Computer Knowledge


7. Environment awareness


8. Current economic affairs


9. Sports


10. Innovations and inventions in Science
