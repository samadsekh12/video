West Bengal All Govt & Competitive Exams General Knowledge (GK) Ultimate Chapterwise Syllabus

--------------------------------------------------------------
1. Geography (ভারত ও পশ্চিমবঙ্গ ভূগোল)
--------------------------------------------------------------
- ভারতের States, Capitals, Union Territories, International Borders
- পশ্চিমবঙ্গের জেলা, জেলা সদর, District History, সৃষ্টি সাল
- Map based Locations, Major Cities & Towns
- Important Rivers, Lakes, Waterfalls, Dams (Ganga, Hooghly, Teesta, Damodar, Subarnarekha, Farakka)
- Hills, Plateaus, Darjeeling Himalaya, Ajodhya, Chhotanagpur, Eastern Bengal ranges
- Sundarbans Delta, Mangrove Forests, Biosphere Reserve
- Forests, Conservation Acts, Ramsar Sites
- National Parks, Wildlife/Bird Sanctuaries (Sundarbans, Jaldapara, Neora Valley, Gorumara, Ballavpur)
- Soils, Climate, Rainfall patterns, Agricultural zones, Major crops (Rice, Jute, Tea, Potato)
- Minerals (Coal, Iron, Limestone, Copper), ও ক্ষেত্র (Raniganj, Durgapur)
- Major Industries: Jute, Tea, IT, Steel, Fishery, Petrochemicals, Cement, Rice Mill, Power, Agriculture
- Ports: Kolkata, Haldia, Inland Waterways
- Important Tourism/Heritage sites, Religious places, Festivals, Melas (Gangasagar, Poush, Kumbh)

--------------------------------------------------------------
2. History (ইতিহাস - ভারত এবং বাংলা)
--------------------------------------------------------------
- Ancient: Indus, Vedic, Maurya, Gupta, Pala, Sena, Chola, Bengal Sultanate
- Medieval: Mughal, Nawabs, Portuguese, Dutch, British arrival
- Modern: Plassey, Buxar, British Rule, Policy, Bengali Freedom/Social Reform Movements
- Bengal Renaissance: Vidyasagar, Rabindranath, Bankim, Kazi Nazrul, Aurobindo
- Partition of Bengal (1905, 1947): Causes, Movement, Impact (বঙ্গভঙ্গ, দেশভাগ)
- Indigo, Santal, Chuar, Tebhaga, Naxalbari, Women's movements
- Swadeshi Movement, Boycott, Civil Disobedience, Quit India
- Principal Revolutionaries: Khudiram, Bagha Jatin, Pritilata, Matangini, Dinesh, Benoy-Badal, Chittagong Armoury Raid
- Netaji Subhas, Gandhiji, Surendranath, Binoy Basu, Bidhan Chandra Roy
- Formation & reorganization of West Bengal
- Social Reformers: Raja Rammohan, Keshub Chandra, Gopal Krishna Gokhale
- Important Monuments: Victoria Memorial, Hazarduari, Bishnupur, Belur Math, Dakshineswar, Shantiniketan
- Bengal in WWI/WWII, পোষ্ট-ইন্ডিপেনডেন্সে বাংলা রাজনীতি
- Women's emancipation, Bengal’s literary & social movements

--------------------------------------------------------------
3. Polity & Constitution (সংবিধান, প্রশাসন, সরকার/নিতি)
--------------------------------------------------------------
- Indian Constitution: Framing & Features
- Fundamental Rights/Duties, Directive Principles
- President, Vice President, PM, Governor, CM, Cabinet, Ministers
- Parliament & Assemblies: Lok Sabha, Rajya Sabha, Vidhan Sabha/Parishad
- Judiciary: Supreme Court, Calcutta High Court, District Courts
- Panchayati Raj, Urban Local Bodies, Corporation, Municipality, Gram Panchayat
- Election process, Voter rights, Polling system, EVM
- Reservation Policy, Welfare Schemes
- Schedules, Articles, State Symbols, State Emblem
- Major Committees/Commissions
- Local Government & Administration, Police system
- RTI, Child Marriage, Women & Child Welfare Laws

--------------------------------------------------------------
4. Economy (অর্থনীতি/শিল্প/আর্থ সামাজিক)
--------------------------------------------------------------
- Indian Economy: Basic Concepts, Sectors
- WB Economy: GDP, Agro-based, Major & Small Industries, MSME, Cooperative Movement
- Livelihoods, Fisheries, Cottage Industries
- Banking sector: RBI, Major Banks, Financial Inclusion, Microfinance, KCC, ATM
- Government Economic Schemes: MGNREGA, PM Kisan, National Food Security, Crop Insurance, Poverty Alleviation, SHG
- Trade, Export-Import, Ports, Railways, Industrial Corridors
- Statistics: Poverty, Unemployment, HDI, Literacy
- State Budget, Taxation basics, GST, Economic Survey
- Planning Commission/NITI Aayog

--------------------------------------------------------------
5. Science, Tech & Environment (বিজ্ঞান-প্রযুক্তি-পরিবেশ)
--------------------------------------------------------------
- Physics, Chemistry, Biology, General Science basics
- Human Body, Disease, Vaccine, Nutrition, Genetics, Blood Group
- Plant Science, Photosynthesis, Ecology, Biodiversity, Conservation
- Pollution (Air/Water/Soil/Noise), Environmental Movements (Chipko, Bishnoi), Afforestation
- Natural Disasters in Bengal: Flood, Cyclone, Earthquake, Amphan, Aila
- Sundarbans ecology, Conservation efforts
- Indian/Bengali Scientists: S N Bose, J C Bose, Meghnad Saha, Prafulla Ray, Acharya Jagadish
- Innovations, Nobel Awards in Science, Green Revolution
- Space Research: ISRO, PSLV, Chandrayaan, Gaganyaan
- Computer basics, Internet, Cyber Security, Digital India & Bengal, Mobile Apps

--------------------------------------------------------------
6. Society, Culture & Religion (সমাজ-সংস্কৃতি-ধর্ম)
--------------------------------------------------------------
- Bengali Language, Dialects, Scripts (Bangla, Santali, Nepali, Urdu)
- Major Festivals: Durga, Kali, Saraswati, Nabanna, Poila Baisakh, Gangasagar, Eid, Christmas, Rath
- Literature: Nobel Laureates, Rabindranath, Nazrul, Bankim, Sarat, Michael, Sukumar Ray
- Folk Dance/Music: Baul, Bhatiali, Chau, Raibeshe
- Bengali Theatre, Cinema, TV, Opera, Drama, Jatra
- Cuisine, Attire, Art Forms (Terracotta, Kantha, Alpona, Patachitra, Clay Pottery)
- Libraries, Museums, Archives, Literary Institutions
- Customs, Social/Family Practices, Rituals, Bengali Calendar months
- Prominent Social reformers/activists/women achievers in Bengal  
- Women's movements, Educational movements, Child Welfare

- Sports Events, Venues (Eden Gardens), State/National Sports Day
- Arjuna Award, Dronacharya, Khel Ratna
